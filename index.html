? Khada Opus — Quick Manual
🎬 Getting Started
Paste a YouTube URL on the landing screen and click Load → to open a video. Or click start editing without a video to work on subtitles alone. Use ↩ New in the topbar to go back to the landing screen.
📐 Layout Overview
Top-left — Effects panel. Drag an effect card onto a subtitle block on the timeline to apply it.
Center — Video preview with playback controls below it.
Timeline — Your subtitle blocks live here on tracks. Drag to move, drag edges to resize.
Right — Inspector (style the selected block) + Subtitle list below it.
Every divider bar between panels is draggable to resize panels.
✏️ Editing Subtitles
Click + Add Block or press N to add a subtitle at the playhead position.
Click any timeline block to select it — the Inspector opens on the right where you can edit text, font, colors, position on screen, shadow, and fade.
Press Q to set the block's start to the playhead. Press E to set its end.
Press Delete to remove the selected block. Shift+click to select multiple blocks.
▶ Playback & Timeline
Press Space to play/pause. Click the ruler at the top of the timeline to jump the playhead.
Click any subtitle in the Subtitle list panel to jump the playhead to it.
Snap makes block edges snap to the nearest second grid. Magnet snaps to other block edges.
Use the zoom slider or +/− to zoom the timeline in and out.
✦ Effects Panel
The Effects panel (top-left) lists available effects you can apply to subtitle blocks.
To apply an effect — drag the effect card from the panel and drop it onto a subtitle block on the timeline.
Once applied, a badge appears on the block indicating the effect is active. Click that badge to open the effect's editor, which replaces the Inspector while you're editing it.
Close the effect editor with ✕ to return to the regular Inspector.
↩ Undo / Redo
Every action is undoable — moving blocks, adding, deleting, style changes, and more.
Press Ctrl+Z to undo, Ctrl+Y to redo. Up to 80 steps of history.
💾 Exporting
Click Export ↓ in the topbar to download your subtitle file.
.YTT — keeps all styling, positioning, multi-track, and effects. Upload in YouTube Studio → Subtitles → Add → Upload file (With timing).
.SRT / .VTT — plain text only, no styling. Works in most video players.
⌨ Shortcuts & Keybinds
Click the ⌨ button in the topbar to see all shortcuts and rebind any key. Changes save automatically in your browser.
⌨ Keyboard Shortcuts
Click a key to rebind · Press the new key combo
